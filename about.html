About Manuel Bussmann

My Story

I learned design because I was tired of spending countless hours on a web design that looked mediocre and didn't perform well. During more than 50 intensive mentoring sessions and 9 months of project-based online design training I learned to design more minimalistic and pixel accurate interfaces. But only the design of consistent looking interfaces with the use of UI patterns, style guides and design systems did not satisfy me. One important part was missing, which was usability.

I continued to improve my skills and began to take online UX training courses along with more mentoring sessions. As a UX designer, my core discipline is design thinking. To manage design thinking, I use the double diamond methodology. Within the double diamond I go through diverging and converging phases to achieve a satisfactory result.
